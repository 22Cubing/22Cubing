What is a Rubik's Cube?

A Rubik's Cube is a 3D puzzle invented by Ern≈ë Rubik in 1974. It has six faces, each covered with nine stickers in different colors. The goal is to twist and turn the cube until each face has only one color!

Why are Rubik's Cubes so cool?

  • They're fun to play with
  • They help improve your memory and problem-solving skills
  • You can learn cool tricks to impress your friends
  • There are competitions where people solve cubes super fast!

Fun Fact!

Did you know? There are 43,252,003,274,489,856,000 possible ways to arrange the colors on a Rubik's Cube. That's more than 43 quintillion combinations!
